Setup app for Star Wars: Legion Miniatures game

This is an app to aid setup for skirmish games in the Star Wars: Legion miniatures game.

It has had many users from the Star Wars: Legion community and I have recieved mostly positive feedback, while the only negative feedback has been contructive and has helped me to improve the app.

Acknowledgements:
  • Card images are from Star Wars: Legion by Fantasy Flight Games
  • Implements the Fisher-Yates algorithm for shuffling the cards
  • Most of the styling uses w3.css
